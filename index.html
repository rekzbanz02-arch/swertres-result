📖 HOW TO USE THIS APP
🎱
Swertres 3D Lotto Results
Shows today's official PCSO Swertres 3D draw results for the three daily draws. Each ball shows the winning digit. Status badges tell you if a draw is LIVE, UPCOMING, or FINAL. Results auto-refresh every 5 minutes.
3 draws daily 2PM · 5PM · 9PM Prize: ₱4,500
🎲
STL Misamis Oriental Results
Shows today's STL Hexaprime Inc. Swer3 draw results for Misamis Oriental. Three draws are shown per day. Data is sourced live from pcsodraw.com and refreshes automatically.
3 draws daily 10:30AM · 3PM · 8PM STL Swer3
🔮
Daily Predictions
Tap the header to expand. Shows today's algorithmically predicted numbers for both Swertres 3D and STL, generated once per day using weighted frequency analysis of past draws. The confidence bar reflects how strongly recent history favors those digits — not a guarantee of winning. Use ↻ New Prediction to regenerate a fresh set. Tap ✦ Save 3D or ✦ Save STL to store today's prediction.
Freq. Analysis Recency Weighting Hot Streak Detect Entertainment only
🎯
Prediction Hit Tracker
Tap the header to expand. Loads all your previously saved predictions and compares them against actual draw results. A prediction is a ✓ HIT if all three digits appear in any draw that day — in any order (rambolito match). A ✗ MISS means no draw matched. ⌛ PENDING means today's draws aren't all in yet. Use the Refresh button to re-fetch the latest list from Cloud. Switch between Swertres 3D and STL Hexaprime tabs to view each game's history separately.
Any-order match Cloud storage Hit rate tracked
📅
Draw History
Shows the last 30 days of actual draw results for both Swertres 3D and STL. Tap either panel header to expand and scroll through past results. History is cached for 30 minutes to keep the app fast.
Last 30 days Tap to expand Cached 30 min
🔍
Search By Date
Look up draw results for a specific date or a date range. Toggle between Single Date and Date Range mode using the buttons. Results show both Swertres 3D and STL draws for the selected period. Only dates within the last 30 days are available.
Single or range 3D + STL combined 30-day window
💡
Swertres Information
Tap 💡 Swertres Information to expand prize details. Straight (exact order) pays ₱4,500. Rambolito 3 (2 same + 1 different, any order) pays ₱1,500. Rambolito 6 (3 different digits, any order) pays ₱750. All per ₱12 play.
Straight ₱4,500 Rambolito 3 ₱1,500 Rambolito 6 ₱750
